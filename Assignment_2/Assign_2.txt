Assignment Name: AI Around Me

Description:
AI is used in many daily applications. Below are 10 real-life examples along with how Machine Learning (ML) works behind them.


1. Google Maps
   ML analyzes traffic data to suggest the fastest routes and predict travel time.

2. YouTube
   ML recommends videos based on your watch history and preferences.

3. Instagram
   ML shows posts and reels based on your likes, follows, and interactions.

4. Amazon
   ML suggests products based on your browsing and purchase behavior.

5. Netflix
   ML recommends movies and shows based on your viewing patterns.

6. Google Assistant
   ML understands voice commands using speech recognition and NLP.

7. Gmail
   ML filters spam emails and suggests smart replies.

8. Spotify
   ML creates playlists based on your listening habits.

9. Flipkart
   ML recommends products and shows personalized deals.

10. Google Photos
    ML recognizes faces and objects to organize photos automatically.
