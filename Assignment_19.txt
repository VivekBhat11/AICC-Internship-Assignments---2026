Assignment Name: Text Challenges

Description:
This assignment focuses on identifying issues in messy text data like slang, emojis, and typos, and understanding how preprocessing improves text quality for machine learning.

---

Issues Identified

 Slang: bro, luv, gr8, omg, lol, thx, ur
 Emojis: 😂 😊 🔥 😴 ❤️ 😋
 Typos: amazng, waait, gud, awsomee
 Short forms: tmrw, rn, asap

---

Preprocessing Needed

1. Convert text to lowercase
2. Remove emojis
3. Correct spelling mistakes
4. Expand short forms (e.g., “ur” → “your”)
5. Remove extra letters (sooo → so)
6. Remove unnecessary symbols

---

 🧠 Conclusion

Text preprocessing makes data clean and structured, which improves the performance of NLP models.
